We plan luxury destination weddings and celebrations across the globe.

From vineyards to medieval castles,town squares, exotic beaches, villas and elegant hotels,
we have a network of some of the greatest places in the world to throw a grand bash. Tell us
the occasion and we’ll tell you a number of great places to host it.

With Resh & Co, you can expect to be pampered with the best of services.

Florence

From the Renaissance to being one of the most important ports of Medieval Europe, Florence is teeming with culture, history and spectacular gothic architecture. Whether it’s a majestic castle or quaint public squares, you can find an ethereal setting in this historic city for your big day.

Tuscany

A land immortalised by art and literature, Tuscany comprises everything that dreams are made of. With breathtaking rolling hills, romantic vineyards, and picturesque villages, Tuscany is an idyllic spot to mark your new journey.

Sicily

With ocean-view panoramas that seem to never end, this South Italian island is a still-hidden gem. Located in the Mediterranean, Sicily is full of untouched emerald green land, known for its mouth-watering seafood and delectable desserts. You can choose between luxury hotels on cliffs, hidden medieval castles, or private residences facing Vulcan Etna.

Lakecomo

Made more famous in recent years by big-name celebrities, Lake Como is one of the dreamiest locations to get married in. If you are looking for a true fairytale wedding, Lake Como, with its exquisite food, wine, and luxurious villas by a shimmering lake in between tall mountains, is just the destination for you!

Bangalore

The high-tech capital of India, Bangalore presents a heady mix of the old world charm and new age design. Known for its buzzing nightlife, and ideal year-round weather, Bangalore is also home to palaces and forts from the 18th and 19th centuries.

Goa

A wonderful aspect of this city, is the juxtaposition of locations it offers--from warm sandy beaches and centuries old churches, to adrenaline fueling water sports and world-famous nightlife. Even though Goa is known for being where the party is at, there are plenty of spots to throw an intimate, dreamy wedding

Kerala

Aptly called God's Own Country, Kerala is full of pristine shores, dense coconut groves and quiet lakes. If the natural scenery isn't enough, sink your palates into one of the most succulent cuisines in all of India.

Rajasthan

You cannot think of the “Big Fat Indian Wedding” without picturing the overarching palaces of Rajasthan. Full of spectacular views and important landmarks--from awe-inspiring mahals to the quaint countryside, there's a lot of places for you to choose from.

Phuket

The private beaches of this Thailand Island offers guests the sweet smell of the ocean, luscious tropical trees, and gorgeous sunset views. Walking down Thalang Road or visiting the exquisite Baan Chinpracha mansion, you can see that Phuket represents the best of Thai and Portuguese architecture and is one of the most luxurious yet serene places to throw your big event.

Krabi

With attractions including hot springs, sea caves, flourishing coral reefs, exotic marine life and limestone cliffs, one can easily spend weeks in Krabi and leave yearning for more. This province is famous for its scenic views, white beaches, and breathtaking sunsets that can best be enjoyed from one of the many beachside bars and restaurants.

Bangkok

The Thais are known to be some of the most friendliest people. Bangkok is full of picture-perfect places to host your big event. Right from the choice of cuisines to the decor and managing local vendors, we'll take care of it while you kick back and enjoy.

Koh-Samui

From its beaches that are dotted with palm trees, to the dense coconut groves, to a wealth of luxury resorts, Koh Samui is full of natural and manmade spectacles. With our established vendor connections, we're in a commanding position to throw you an event better than you can even expect.

Turkey

In a land that's part Asia and part Europe, you can expect not just a fusion of cultures and cuisines, but also a fusion of architecture and locales. Let us find a heartwarming location for the wedding of your dreams.

Bali

Not only is Bali known for its yoga and meditation retreats, it's also the perfect place to start a new chapter. From the dense mountains and rice paddies to the serene waters and opulent resorts, Bali is full of life, vigour and fun.

Maldives

Sugary white sand beaches, crystalline waters and endless sunshine – the Maldives is an incredible destination for an effortlessly romantic wedding blessing.

Sri-lanka

Called "the pearl of the Indian Ocean", Sri Lanka is full of untouched land, amazing cuisine and gregarious people. If you're looking to get married in a small village square or in one of the many beaches on this Island, this is the place to make that happen.
